## HOW THE PROGRAMME WORKS

### Section Heading
From Operations → To System-Level & Strategic Thinking

**01 — Understand Business Operations**
How products move across systems. The role of supply chains in business performance. Cost, service, and efficiency drivers — and why understanding these fundamentals shapes every decision that follows.

**02 — Design Efficient & Scalable Systems**
Move from understanding operations to designing them. Network design, procurement strategy, warehouse management, and inventory planning — approached as interconnected systems, not isolated functions.

**03 — Manage Cost, Risk & Business Trade-offs**
Supply chain decisions are business decisions. Learn to evaluate risk and disruption, optimize for cost and service simultaneously, and understand the financial consequences of operational choices.

**04 — Apply Through Real Scenarios**
Case studies, simulation-driven exercises, ERP tool projects, and capstone work that mirrors real procurement, production, and distribution challenges. Concept-first, application-focused throughout.
