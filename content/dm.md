## HOW THE PROGRAMME WORKS

### Section Heading
From Learning → To Simulation → To Industry Application

**01 — Build Core Business Foundation**
Understand management fundamentals. Learn how businesses function across domains. Prepare for marketing specialization with a grounded sense of how commercial decisions are made.

**02 — Learn Digital Marketing Concepts**
Concept-first, platform-second learning. Understand the strategic logic behind channels, funnels, and campaigns before diving into execution. Frameworks before tools.

**03 — Practice with Stukent Simulations**
Apply what you've learned in structured, real-business-like environments. Stukent-based campaign simulations put you in the seat of a marketing decision-maker — with data, budgets, and outcomes to manage.

**04 — Apply Through Projects and Internship**
Hands-on project work, a structured internship for real-world exposure, and a capstone experience that translates learning into portfolio-ready capability.
